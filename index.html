Who We Are

Welcome to Right Choice Recovery, a trusted recovery and support services provider located at 12 Stults Rd #137, Dayton, NJ 08810. Dedicated to helping individuals and families navigate the challenges of addiction and mental health recovery, Right Choice Recovery offers compassionate, professional guidance every step of the way. Their experienced team is committed to providing personalized care and evidence-based solutions tailored to each client's unique needs. Whether you are just beginning your recovery journey or seeking continued support, Right Choice Recovery is here to help you make the right choice for a healthier, brighter future.

What We Do

Right Choice Recovery stands as a beacon of hope for those struggling with addiction and co-occurring mental health conditions in the New Jersey area. Their comprehensive approach to recovery encompasses a wide range of services designed to address the physical, emotional, and psychological aspects of addiction. By combining proven therapeutic techniques with individualized care plans, the team ensures that every client receives the attention and resources necessary to achieve lasting sobriety and wellness.

The professionals at Right Choice Recovery bring years of experience and specialized training to their work, allowing them to address a broad spectrum of substance use disorders and behavioral health challenges. Their staff includes licensed counselors, certified recovery specialists, and support personnel who work collaboratively to create a nurturing and structured environment. This multidisciplinary approach ensures that clients benefit from well-rounded care that supports recovery from every angle.

Right Choice Recovery understands that no two recovery journeys are alike, which is why they place a strong emphasis on developing personalized treatment strategies for each individual. From initial assessments and intervention services to ongoing counseling and aftercare planning, every stage of the recovery process is handled with the utmost care and professionalism. Clients and their families can expect open communication, consistent support, and a genuine commitment to long-term success.

Conveniently situated in Dayton, NJ, Right Choice Recovery serves clients throughout the surrounding communities and greater New Jersey region. Their accessible location, combined with a welcoming and judgment-free environment, makes it easier for individuals to take that critical first step toward recovery. The team works diligently to remove barriers to treatment and ensure that quality care is available to all who need it, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Choosing the right recovery partner can make all the difference in achieving a successful and sustainable outcome. Right Choice Recovery has built a strong reputation for delivering results-driven care with empathy, integrity, and professionalism.
